PROJECT GIVINGCARE

Supporting caregivers through knowledge, community and care.

Project GivingCare is a student-led community initiative from the NUS Yong Loo Lin School of Medicine. We work to improve caregiver awareness, preparedness and access to practical support.

ABOUT US

No caregiver should have to navigate the journey alone.

Project GivingCare was established in 2022 as a Local Community Involvement Project under NUS Medicine. Our work centres on the realities caregivers face: emotional strain, limited awareness of support, complex systems and the need to plan ahead.

We bring together students, caregivers, community organisations and healthcare professionals to make caregiving information easier to understand and more practical to use.

WHAT WE DO

Awareness, community and practical education.

♥

Raise awareness

We help current and future caregivers recognise the demands of caregiving and understand that seeking support is part of good care.

↗

Improve access

We organise resources by need so caregivers can more easily find services, financial support, training and community help.

✓

Build preparedness

We support earlier conversations about home care, legal planning, future transitions, palliative care and caregiver wellbeing.

THE CAREGUIDE

A free digital resource hub for caregivers in Singapore.

CareGuide brings practical, evidence-informed information into one accessible place. It covers planning ahead, home care, burnout, communication, palliative care, grief and support for children with special needs.
