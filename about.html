Project GivingCare

Project GivingCare is a Local Community Involvement Project under NUS Medicine, established in 2022.

We aim to improve caregiver awareness, preparedness and access to practical support. Our work recognises that caregivers may be supporting older adults, people with serious illness, individuals with disability or children with special needs.

Our mission

To empower current and future caregivers to better understand caregiving, navigate available support and sustain their own wellbeing.

How we work

We collaborate with caregivers, healthcare professionals and community organisations to create educational programmes, public engagement activities and accessible resources.
